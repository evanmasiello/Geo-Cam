About

Geo Cam is a photo sharing platform that is focused on allowing users to engage with their local communities.













Welcome to Geo Cam!

Geo Cam is a photo sharing app that strives to allow you to better engage with the people around you.

You can see pictures posted near where you are and can post pictures for people near you to see.

You can like and comment on pictures to engage with other users. We hope you have fun trying it out!
